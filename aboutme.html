About Me




Hi, I'm Anne.

I have a B.A. in Psychology from the University of British
Columbia, and 3 years of private tutoring experience in Canada.
I was born in Seattle, in the United States. I moved to Canada
when I was 19, and have travelled around Europe and Southeast Asia.
I have recently moved to Toulouse. I love teaching because I love
meeting new people, and seeing my students' enthusiasm and progress.
It also allows me to work and experience life in other countries, so I
can continue travelling!

My background in both psychology and education allows me to plan
effective and enjoyable lessons for my students. I don't just teach out of
textbooks. I believe the most effective way to learn languages is through
conversation with native speakers, and to personalize each lesson to the
student's needs and goals.

I also have personal experience learning another language. I studied French in school, and am now living in France to improve my language skills. This means I can relate to the challenges and benefits that come with language learning.
